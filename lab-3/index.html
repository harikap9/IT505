Web Technology Serving fonts

I chose fonts from Google Fonts for their variety and reliability across devices. For the heading, I used "Playwrite DE Grund"; for paragraphs, "Edu AU VIC WA NT Dots"; and for the footer, "Playfair Display Dots." After selecting each font, I copied the link tag into the head section of my HTML for easy use in CSS. I also customized font size, weight, and text alignment for improved readability. Google Fonts ensures consistent availability, unlike local fonts.
