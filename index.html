Abstract

The paper looked at issues concerning choice of career among students. After a brief look at what career choice and development is, the paper describes some terms associated with career; terms that are considered synonymous by many but are actually different. The paper is based upon the system theory which focuses on the importance of society and the environment along with individual differences, and it is also based upon the chaos theory which focuses on the complexities of social realities and the unpredictable incidents they will generate. Furthermore, the paper discusses various assumptions or perspectives underlying career choice, such as the belief that opportunities and choices should be available for all people, regardless of sex, socio-economic class, religion, disability, sexual orientation, age, or cultural background.
