Abstract

The paper looked at issues concerning choice of career among students. After a brief look at what career choice and development is, the paper describes some terms associated with career; terms that are considered synonymous by many but are actually different. Furthermore, the paper discusses various assumptions or perspectives underlying career choice, such as the belief that opportunities and choices should be available for all people.

Career development

Career development (in the context of career orientation) is a lifelong process which involves the management of learning, work and transition in order to advance and head towards a determined individual future developed personally.

Conclusion

The article highlights the challenges of finding work in Nigeria, especially for recent graduates. However, despite the difficulties, it emphasizes that with careful planning and self-awareness, there are opportunities available in various fields, including government work and the growing technology sector.
